About IX Lab ✨

We are looking for passionate students interested in solving real-life issues from the perspective of human-computer interaction (HCI). IX Lab aims to quantitatively measure and evaluate the invisible interactions between humans and computers based on data, ultimately striving to optimize these interactions. We prioritize building and advancing each student's individual research area in the field of human-computer interaction. We welcome students who enjoy raising questions about phenomena that others might overlook and engaging in discussions to solve these problems.
